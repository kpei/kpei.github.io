If you go to a concert and every band plays the exact same song, it doesn't matter how well they play, objectively "performance" would be quite boring. The same analogy applies for osu! No one wants to see the same 10 scores on every profile.

and the length bonus is removed from the PP calculations. A map's star rating is weighted equally for it's entire length during calculations. The existing PP system takes a map for only it's hardest parts and adds them up with exponential decay (similar to how PP is calculated on your profile page). By the time you are past the 10 hardest parts, which sometimes only spans a hard jump section, the remaining beatmap only account for about ~35% of the PP. Furthermore, Star Rating also now scales with length of the map linearly. If you were to copy and paste a map to double it's length, the aim and speed star rating would increase by 10% and 7.5% respectively.

  • Quaver [Fiery's Extra] +DT: 8.73* -> 8.05*
